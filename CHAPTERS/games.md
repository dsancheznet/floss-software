## 0 A.D.

<img style="max-width: 340px;" src="https://play0ad.com/wp-content/gallery/screenshots/EgyptianPyramids.jpg">

> 0 A.D. is a free, open-source, historical Real Time Strategy (RTS) game currently under development by Wildfire Games, a global group of volunteer game developers. As the leader of an ancient civilization, you must gather the resources you need to raise a military force and dominate your enemies.

[Homepage](https://play0ad.com/)

## Glest

<img style="max-width: 340px;" src="http://glest.org/images/galeria/pantallas/s06.jpg">

> Glest is a free 3D real-time strategy game, where you control the armies of two different factions: Tech, which is mainly composed of warriors and mechanical devices, and Magic, that prefers mages and summoned creatures in the battlefield. 

[Homepage](http://glest.org/)

## The dark mod

<img style="max-width: 340px;" src="https://www.thedarkmod.com/wp-content/gallery/environments/thedarkmod-2018-05-26-00-20-43.jpg">

> FPS video game, inspired by the Thief series by Looking Glass Studios. The game provides the basic framework and tools (engine, assets, models, and editor) for more than 100 fan-made missions, including several multi-mission campaigns. The Dark Mod was first released in 2009 as a total conversion mod for Doom 3. Version 2.0 was released in October 2013 as a standalone game using the open-source id Tech 4 game engine. 

[Homepage](https://www.thedarkmod.com/)
